About Me

The path that brought me here

Gloria（范诗雨 in Chinese）

M.A. Candidate in Intercultural Communication in Sports · Beijing Sport University

I'm Gloria (Fan Shiyu), a second-year Master's candidate at Beijing Sport University, majoring in Intercultural Communication in Sports.

My academic journey began with a Bachelor's in English Teaching, where I picked up a bit of education, psychology, and linguistics — though, admittedly, much of it has quietly faded. A love for the Olympic Games inspired me to pivot toward sports for my graduate studies. It was a leap of passion, and one I have never looked back on.

My research focuses on sport and public relations. I'm also deeply interested in international sports exchange, the sports diplomacy policies of different nations, and intercultural communication. I have had the privilege of participating in several major international sporting events, experiences that have reinforced my conviction: sport is a universal thread that connects people across borders. I believe in its quiet power to foster empathy, bridge difference, and, in its own way, make the world a little better.
